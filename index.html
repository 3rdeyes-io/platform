How it works

From weather forecast
to trade alert — automatically.

Every step is logged and published. You always know exactly why a signal fired — or didn't.

01
🛰️
40 cities scanned every 5 minutes
Our AI continuously pulls professional-grade weather data — the same models meteorologists use — for 40 US cities. It runs 24/7 so you don't have to watch anything.
02
🔬
Every forecast model must agree
We run dozens of independent weather simulations across four major forecast models (GFS, ECMWF, ICON, GEM). Every single one must predict the temperature stays safely away from the threshold. One disagreement kills the trade. No signal fires on a split verdict.
03
📏
Safety margin + price check
The forecast must clear the threshold by a comfortable margin (≥3°F inland, ≥5°F coastal) — not just barely. The contract price must also be in the right range. All checks pass → a calibrated win-probability is assigned.
04
⚡
Alert hits your Telegram instantly
You get the city, contract, direction, win-probability, and plain-English reasoning — exactly why the model fired. Place the trade manually on your platform, or let the pipeline handle it automatically.
05
🛡️
Live monitoring + automatic stop-loss
Open positions are watched in real time. You get an alert if a trade moves against you, and an auto-close triggers if it drops below the stop-loss threshold. Each trade is independent — one loss doesn't affect the others.
