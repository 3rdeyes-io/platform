Track record · under reconstruction

We took this page down on purpose.

While auditing our own numbers, we found that the trade ledger published here was under-recording losing trades. The win/loss record shown previously was therefore not accurate.

Rather than display figures we cannot fully stand behind, we have taken the track record offline and are rebuilding it directly from Kalshi's own settlement records — the only fully verifiable source, where every trade, win and loss, is documented by the exchange itself.

Honesty matters more to us than a number on a page. The corrected, exchange-verified record will be published here when it is ready.
