Track record · under reconstruction

We took this page down on purpose.

While reconciling our trade records, we found our data sources disagree on how stop-loss exits are counted. Rather than publish a win-rate figure we cannot fully stand behind, we have taken the record offline.

We are rebuilding it directly from Kalshi's own settlement records, the only fully verifiable source, where every trade (win, loss, and stop-loss exit alike) is documented by the exchange itself.

Honesty matters more to us than a number on a page. The corrected, exchange-verified record will be published here when it is ready.
