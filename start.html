Quick-Start Guide

From sign-up to your first trade.

Five simple steps. No trading experience needed - and you always stay in full control of your own account and funds.

1

Get the signals (free)

Drop your email on the home page, or join our free Telegram channel directly. Every signal tells you the city, the exact contract, the model's win-probability, and the NO price to pay.

2

Open a trading account

Our signals settle on Kalshi, a CFTC-regulated exchange. The same temperature contracts also trade on Coinbase and Robinhood - use whichever app you prefer (see where to trade). You fund and control your own account; 3rd Eyes never touches your money.

3

Wait for a signal

We only send one when all four weather models agree and the market is mispriced - that is roughly 4-8 a month, not dozens a day. Quiet weather means no signal, and that is by design: quality over quantity.

4

Place the trade

Open the contract in your app, buy NO at or below the listed price, and you are done. Place it, set it down, get on with your day - most markets settle the same or the next day.

5

Size it sensibly and track results

Start small. Use the Sizing & Risk guide to choose a stake and understand the stop-loss, and never stake more than you can afford to lose. Every trade we publish is verifiable on the public Track Record. When you are ready for full coverage, upgrade to Pro.

Need a hand?

Email us at support@3rdeyes.io or message us on Telegram. We usually reply within a day. New to prediction markets? The methodology explains exactly how the signals are built.
