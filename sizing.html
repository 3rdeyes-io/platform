What happens if a trade goes wrong?

Every position is monitored every 30 seconds. If the NO price drops from 95¢ to 85¢ (rare weather event emerging), you get an immediate Telegram alert. If it reaches 82¢, the position is automatically closed - regardless of how much time is left.

85¢ threshold
Telegram warning alert fires
82¢ threshold
Position closed. Slot resets to $80.
Other 3 slots
Completely unaffected. Untouched.

Losses do occur. The hard position-size caps exist so a single bad trade can't erase a long run of wins. Specific historical loss figures will be republished once the track record reconstruction (see /track) is complete.
