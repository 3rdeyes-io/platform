What happens if a trade goes wrong?

Every position is monitored every 30 seconds, and the stop sits a fixed 20¢ below your entry - wide enough to ride out intraday noise but not a genuine forecast miss. On a 95¢ buy: a slip to ~85¢ fires an immediate Telegram alert, and a fall to 75¢ auto-closes the position - capping the loss at roughly 20¢ per contract (~21%), regardless of how much time is left.

Losses do occur. The hard position-size caps exist so a single bad trade can't erase a long run of wins. Every individual loss (date, market, P&L) is listed in the full per-trade ledger at /track.
